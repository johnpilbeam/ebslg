Council

EBSLG is directed by a Council which consists of a president, a secretary, a treasurer, a projects coordinator, and the coordinators of each of the three regional groups: Continental; Northern European; and Anglophone. Each of the three regional groups meets annually to consider new applications for membership, initiate discussion on topics of interest specific to the regional members, and make propositions for matters to be discussed at the Annual General Meeting.

Members of the Council can be reached via the ‘Contact us’ page.
