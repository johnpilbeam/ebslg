Directory of members

Membership of the Group

The EBSLG member libraries are divided into three regional groups: the UK and Ireland, Continental, and Northern European regional groups. Membership is limited to a single institutional representative. EBSLG members will be qualified librarians or directors, or hold an equivalent level qualification in a discipline related to librarianship.

Membership of the EBSLG is through application to the regional group coordinators, approved by Council, and then ratified yearly via the joint approval of the Group membership at the Annual General Meeting of the EBSLG.
